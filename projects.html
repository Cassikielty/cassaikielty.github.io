Geospatial Assessment of Environmental Risk

Used TRI data and GIS tools to assess risk to human health from toxic release facilities in high-risk zones. Created spatial maps and statistical outputs for visualization and reporting.

California Climate Trend Visualization

Analyzed NASA GISTEMP data and created animated time-enabled raster layers in ArcGIS Pro. Visualized long-term climate trends and extracted zonal statistics for regional analysis.

Collected field data on butterfly and plant species to map biodiversity and interaction patterns. Used GIS to identify spatial relationships and develop ecological insights.

Camera Trap Wildlife Study

Used XLMiner and historical field data to assess wildlife species distribution before and after hurricane events. Integrated environmental datasets to analyze temporal changes.
